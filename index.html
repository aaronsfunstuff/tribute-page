Xi Jinping

Timeline of Xi Jingping

1953: Xi Jinping is born on June 15th in Beijing, China.

1975: Joins the Communist Party of China (CPC).

1999: Becomes Governor of Fujian province.

2007: Joins the Politburo Standing Committee (PSC) and is appointed Party Secretary of Shanghai.

2010: Becomes Vice President of China and Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission (CMC).

2013: Assumes the presidency of China and becomes Chairman of the Central Military Commission, consolidating power quickly.

2017: "Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era" is added to the Party Constitution.

2021: Introduces "Common Prosperity" policies focusing on reducing inequality and promoting social welfare.
